# Insight Distribution System

![Insight Distribution System](https://oss.javaguide.cn/github/javaguide/books/deep-understand-of-distributed-system.png)

**[In-depth Understanding of Distributional Systems](https://book.douban.com/subject/35794814/)** is an original book in distributed Chinese that was published in 2022, focusing on basic concepts in distributed fields, common challenges, and consensus algorithms.

The author uses a lot of space to introduce the very important consensus algorithm in the distributed field, and it's also based on Go, the novel algorithm that brought you a consensus algorithm from zero.

Honestly, I haven't started reading this book. But! I've read almost every one of the distributed articles on the book's author's blog. Since 2019, the author has been working on the In-depth Understanding Distribution System, and in 2020, it took almost two years to finalize the text.

From Paxos to Zookeeper

![From Paxos to Zookeeper](https://oss.javaguide.cn/github/javaguide/books/image-20211216161118.png)

**[From Paxos to Zookeeper](https://book.douban.com/subject/26292004/)** is a good introductory book on distributive theory. The book focuses on several typical distributed consistency agreements, as well as thinking to address the issue of distributed consistency, and focuses on the Paxos and ZAB agreements.

PS: Zookeeper is not used enough to focus on learning, but the Paxos and ZAB protocols are worth a lot of study.
