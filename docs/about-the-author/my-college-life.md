# First year

In my freshman year, I didn't focus on learning programming, mostly extracurricular activities.

Maybe it's because it's a new city, and I was interested in everything around it. Or maybe it's because I was a little obstinate at the time and I didn't have any direction.

This year, I went with a group of new students in my class to visit a number of places in Chuzhou, such as the Jianzhou Museum, the Yangtze Bridge, and the Zhang Dynasty Temple.
