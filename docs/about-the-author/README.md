In this article, I will briefly introduce myself through Q&A.

When did I graduate?

A lot of old readers should know better. I graduated from undergraduate studies in '19 and just graduated to a foreign firm.

My school background is worse; I struggled in high school and barely passed with a score of 20 to go to a very common two-and-a-half-year program in Jianzhou. It is, however, a good thing that I have not given up on my education, but rather have had to work harder than my fellow students, and that the university experience as a whole has been better.

How long have I been writing blogs?

That's fast! I started my own blog in my junior year. It was then that I posted my own learning notes and my own programs on a blog platform. For example, this article was summarized against teaching materials when I was studying computer networks in my second year.

A lot of young people around me often ask me, "Is it too late for me to write a blog?"

I feel like, ha! If you want to do something, try not to be late and ask yourself if it's worth it. Life is wonderful, and every major decision we make has an impact on our future course of life. Good or bad, only we know!

For me, this decision to insist on blogging has had a very positive impact on my life course! So I also recommend that you develop a habit of blogging.
