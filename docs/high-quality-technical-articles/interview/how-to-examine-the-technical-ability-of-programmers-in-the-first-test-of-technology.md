What's the technology base?

How do you look at the technology base as a technical pilot? What exactly is the technology base? Know what or how to think? Knowledge, as an existing system of mature principles, forms an important part of the foundation, and it is particularly important to know how to think. As they say, know it, but know it. After all, there is no rule for a procedural world that is essentially logical. Know how to think, and be well designed and developed, to go into the details; that's the basis of technology.

Why look at the technology base?

The two most important technical thinking capabilities of programmers are those of logical thinking and abstract design. Logical thinking capabilities are the foundation, and abstract design capabilities are high. An examination of the technical basis would allow for a simultaneous examination of both mental capacities. The understanding of basic technical concepts and linkages is to look at the ability to think logically; the ability to abstract operational issues into technical issues and a reasonable organizational mapping is to look at the ability to design abstractly.

Most operational issues can be technical in abstraction. Operational issues are, in a sense, only an interdisciplinary formulation of technical issues.

It is therefore possible to examine the true technical strength of the candidate through a technical basis: the depth and breadth of the technology.
