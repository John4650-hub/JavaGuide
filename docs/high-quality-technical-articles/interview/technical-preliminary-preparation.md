# Look at the goals and the thinking

First, it is clear that the objectives of the initial technical test are:

- Technical basis of candidates;
- Candidates' thinking and ability to solve problems.

The technology base is the cornerstone, seven points, and the idea and the ability to solve the problem is to land (the part that emerges from the iceberg), three points. Operational and Technical Basic Study, 3-7.

# Technical fundamentals

Why look at the technology base?

The two most important technical thinking capabilities of programmers are those of logical thinking and abstract design. Logical thinking capabilities are the foundation, and abstract design capabilities are high. An examination of the technical basis would allow for a simultaneous examination of both mental capacities. The understanding of basic technical concepts and linkages is to look at the ability to think logically; the ability to abstract operational issues into technical issues and a reasonable organizational mapping is to look at the ability to design abstractly.

Most operational issues can be technical in abstraction. Operational issues are, in a sense, only an interdisciplinary formulation of technical issues.

As a result, **the real technological strength of the candidate can be seen through a technical base examination: depth and breadth of technology.**

What about the technology base?

What about the technology base? Consider it through an effective, multi-dimensional Questioning Model.

What is it? Why

What is the basic understanding of the concept and why is it the principle of its realization?

For example: What's the index? How has the index been achieved?

## Guide - Horizontal Question - In-depth Question

Guidance, like, "Are you familiar with Java Synchronization Tool?" A test, with a positive answer, could ask further: “What are the types of synchronized tools you know?” Knowing the breadth of the candidates;

After obtaining the candidate's answer, you can ask further: “Tell us about the principles of `Concurrent HashMap' or `AQS'?”

The extent to which a person can articulate the principles of technology, including thinking and detail, illustrates how well he possesses it.

### Jump/ Cross-Question

For example, when it comes to efficient search, you can talk about the Hash Algorithm. There are links and many differences. It is also a technical approach.

## Concluding questions

For example, what lessons can you share in doing XXX? Examination of candidate's capacity to summarize.

# It's real and theoretical

For example, when candidates describe the JVM memory model layout, they can then ask: What causes might lead to OOM, what precautions are in place, have you ever encountered memory leakage problems, and how to check and resolve such problems?

For example, when candidates talk about SQL optimization and index optimization, it's about how the index works and how best to build it.

If, for example, the candidate spoke of the matter, then it would be appropriate to talk about the principle of the matter, the level of isolation, the picture, etc.;
