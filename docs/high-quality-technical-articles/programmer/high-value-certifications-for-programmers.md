A certificate is a good thing to prove your ability. It is a symbol of your power. In the short interview time, the certificate can add a lot of points. Upgrading yourself through the test is a very high-value approach. However, there are not many professional qualifications in the IT industry compared to those in the financial, construction, and medical sectors.

Let me summarize some of the common certificates that programmers can take.

# Soft Test

The National Computer Technology and Software Professional Technical Qualification (Standing) Examination, known as the “Soft Examination,” is a highly recognized computer technology qualification in the country. Despite the fact that some people dispute its real value, it is very useful in specific areas and circumstances, such as the high level of recognition of soft-test certificates in state enterprises and the use of soft-test certificates in some cities for crediting households and for tax subsidies.

There are three levels: first, middle, and higher in the soft examinations, which are recommended for direct advanced examinations. It is more difficult to test high for software than for PMP (accreditation of project management professionals), especially in the dissertation section, where most people hang. After a soft test, some units can register internally and receive hundreds more per month.

# PAT

The Elevator Computer Capability Assessment (PAT) is an assessment system that focuses on the ability to study algorithms and is hosted by Zhejiang University. The assessment is divided into four levels: base, level B, level A, and top.

Through PAT, which evaluates and meets the corresponding ratings and scores set by the Union enterprise, it is possible to cross the educational threshold, remove the screening of the curriculum vitae and the written examination, and obtain a direct interview opportunity. Specific companies can visit the website: <https://www.patest.cn/company>.

For students studying at Zhejiang University, PAT (A) grades can be repeated in one year as a master's degree entrance examination.

# PMP

The PMP (Project Management Professional) certification, which is provided by the American Project Management Association (PMI), is the most recognized project management professional certification globally. PMP Certification is designed to upgrade the knowledge and skills of project management professionals to ensure the successful completion of projects.

PMP is a “one hand, global” certification, and PMP certificates still have a higher gold content for project managers. Globally, many successful firms use PMP certification as the entry criterion for project managers.

But! The real value is not the PMP certificate, but the PMBOK project management system, which also includes a large number of project cases in various fields such as business activities, business projects, organizational planning, and construction.

In addition, the PMP certificate is not a large certificate, but a base certificate.

# ACP

The ACP (Agile Certified Practitioner) certification, also provided by the American Project Management Association (PMI), is another important certification in the area of project management. Unlike PMP, which focuses on traditional waterfall approaches, ACP focuses on agile project management approaches such as Scrum, Kanban, Lean, and Extreme Programming (XP).

# OCP

Oracle Certified Professional (OCP) is a professional certification provided by Oracle, focusing on Oracle databases and related technologies. This certification is intended to validate and certify the professional knowledge and skills of individuals in the use and management of the Oracle database.
