It is highly likely that, when interviewing or contacting a side profession, it will be possible to prove its strength convincingly. How can you prove your strength? The most convincing is the background endorsement of big corporate positions, not one of them, for example, in the BAT senior structure, so nothing else needs to be said.

However, not everyone is a big corporate architect as soon as they enter the office, and on their way up, they can also prove themselves by means of public numbers, column books, GitHub code volumes, and books and videos. Compared to other ways, technical books that belong to themselves are relatively better equipped to give recognition to their strength by being held by national publishers, while for some small companies, a book that belongs to itself can even be described as an interview-free pass. So in this paper, we're going to talk to a lot of programmers' friends about publishing technology.

# 1. I don't have the ability to read again, but to do so in the process

I know a lot of my friends; they're in their first book in three years of work, some of them good, even in school.

In contrast to this, many students may wish to wait until technology is accumulated. It's probably less active, writing books, technology, and writing books that help people, which is absolutely possible.

For example, when students learn more about Python data analysis and machine learning, which are more popular recently, they can, after systematic learning, sort out the cases of reptiles, data analysis, and machine learning that they have learned before, and organize them in a way that is appropriate for beginners, according to their own understandings, before they can publish. This type of book may not be very helpful to senior people, but it is absolutely helpful to first-class readers because it contains cases, as it is a personal expression. In other words, if it is not the dynamic of the book, or if the learning process is simple or not necessarily fully committed, the goal of the book is more likely to ensure its effectiveness.

# 2. Books written by advanced developers and architects for primary development

It has also been mentioned that, as an example of initial development suitable for case studies, the Python reptile data analysis machine can first look for a few books that are available in this field, or that contain different chapters, but that should be included in an integrated view. And then you look at other people's books, like a chapter on reptiles, a chapter on pandas, a chapter on matplotlib, and so on, and you can put together several chapters into a book. Anyway, you don't copy what other people's books contain, but you can refer to what other people write.

Once the chapter has been completed, the subsections of each chapter, such as chapter 3 on reptile cases, can be determined by 3.1 talking about reptile concepts, 3.2 about how to build the Scrapy library, 3.3 about how to develop the Scrapy reptile case, and by scraping the order of the preface, the framework of a book can be established. Since it is a casebook, it is a code that is running, and then it is used to teach people how to use it. The case is not necessarily very deep, but it needs to be read by beginners to understand it, and after learning in accordance with the knowledge system you have given, the content of the subject is understood. Moreover, after reading your book, you can acquire knowledge of this field and be able to develop it in basic terms by referencing the cases you give on reptiles, machine learning, etc. This goal, for primary development, should not be difficult to achieve with a little care and time.

And for advanced development and architects, in addition to writing populist casebooks, you can be given in books the development experience that you have learned from big companies, the so-called pedals, such as Python's techniques in setting the axes when using the matplotlib legends, and the common problems that you encounter when setting them, and your books have a higher volume of gold if they contain a large amount of this experience.

In addition, senior developers and architects can write more technical books, such as on high-level and high-level experiences, or k8s+docker on high-level and combined experiences, in which codes can be given, and more so on the implementation of programs and structures, such as high-level and high-profile scenarios, how to pick up options, how to avoid punches, avalanches, how to sort out online Redis questions, how to design a failure response plan.
